<h2><strong>Vangan Waterfall (Ankada Dhodh): A Hidden Monsoon Paradise in Dang, Gujarat</strong></h2><p>Tucked away in the lush forests of the Dang district, <strong>Vangan Waterfall</strong>, popularly known as <strong>Ankada Dhodh</strong>, is one of South Gujarat's hidden natural gems. Surrounded by dense greenery, rolling hills, and untouched landscapes, this beautiful waterfall comes alive during the monsoon season, attracting nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.</p><p>The cascading waters, peaceful atmosphere, and scenic surroundings make Vangan Waterfall a perfect destination for travelers looking to explore the lesser-known wonders of Dang.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Why Visit Vangan Waterfall?</strong></h2><ul><li>A beautiful offbeat waterfall destination.</li><li>Surrounded by lush forests and hills.</li><li>Ideal for monsoon tourism and photography.</li><li>Less crowded than many popular waterfalls.</li><li>Offers a peaceful escape into nature.</li></ul><p><br></p><h2><strong>About Vangan Waterfall (Ankada Dhodh)</strong></h2><p>Vangan Waterfall is a seasonal waterfall that reaches its full beauty during the monsoon months. Rainwater flowing through the hills and forests creates a spectacular cascade that plunges over rocky terrain into natural pools below.</p><p>The waterfall is located amidst the rich natural landscapes of Dang, known for their biodiversity, tribal culture, and scenic beauty.</p><p>Its relatively untouched environment makes it an excellent destination for visitors seeking tranquility and natural charm.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Main Attractions</strong></h2><h3><strong>Scenic Waterfall</strong></h3><p>The waterfall creates a stunning visual spectacle during monsoon, with water flowing through the green hills of Dang.</p><h3><strong>Natural Surroundings</strong></h3><p>Dense forests, mist-covered hills, and fresh mountain air create a refreshing atmosphere throughout the area.</p><h3><strong>Photography Opportunities</strong></h3><p>The dramatic landscapes and flowing water provide excellent opportunities for nature and landscape photography.</p><h3><strong>Peaceful Environment</strong></h3><p>The remote location ensures a calm and relaxing experience away from crowded tourist destinations.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Best Time to Visit</strong></h2><h3><strong>Monsoon (June -
