## Content (English)

<h2><strong>Sarangpur Hanuman Temple: A Sacred Spiritual Destination in Gujarat</strong></h2><p>Located in the Botad district of Gujarat, <strong>Sarangpur Hanuman Temple</strong>, popularly known as <strong>Kashtabhanjan Hanuman Temple</strong>, is one of the most revered Hanuman temples in India. The temple attracts thousands of devotees every day who come seeking blessings, spiritual peace, and relief from difficulties.</p><p>Renowned for its divine atmosphere and rich religious significance, Sarangpur has become an important pilgrimage destination for followers of the Swaminarayan tradition and devotees of Lord Hanuman from across the country.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Why Visit Sarangpur?</strong></h2><ul><li>Home to the famous Kashtabhanjan Hanuman Temple.</li><li>One of Gujarat's most important pilgrimage destinations.</li><li>Known for spiritual healing and devotion.</li><li>Beautiful temple architecture and peaceful surroundings.</li><li>Managed by the Swaminarayan Sampraday.</li></ul><p><br></p><h2><strong>About Kashtabhanjan Hanuman Temple</strong></h2><p>The temple was established by Sadguru Gopalanand Swami, a prominent saint of the Swaminarayan Sampraday. The idol of Lord Hanuman installed here is believed to possess divine powers that help devotees overcome obstacles, fears, and negative influences.</p><p>The name "Kashtabhanjan" means "the remover of sorrows and difficulties," reflecting the faith that devotees place in Lord Hanuman's blessings.</p><p>The temple's spiritual significance and daily rituals create a deeply devotional atmosphere that attracts visitors throughout the year.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Main Attractions</strong></h2><h3><strong>Kashtabhanjan Hanuman Idol</strong></h3><p>The temple's main attraction is the powerful and beautifully adorned idol of Lord Hanuman, worshipped by thousands of devotees daily.</p><h3><strong>Temple Complex</strong></h3><p>The well-maintained complex includes prayer halls, spiritual facilities, accommodation areas, and beautifully landscaped surroundings.</p><h3><strong>Daily Aarti and Rituals</strong></h3><p>Devotees can participate in various religious ceremonies, bhajans, and aartis conducted throughout the day.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Best Time to Visit</strong></h2><h3><strong>October - March</strong></h3><p>The weather remains pleasant, making it ideal for temple visits and sightseeing.</p><h3><strong>Hanuman Jayanti</strong></h3><p>The temple witnesses grand celebrations and attracts a large number of devotees during Hanuman Jayanti.</p><p><strong>Recommended Period:</strong> October to March.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Things to Do in Sarangpur</strong></h2><ul><li>Seek blessings at Kashtabhanjan Hanuman Temple.</li><li>Attend the temple's daily aarti ceremonies.</li><li>Explore the peaceful temple complex.</li><li>Learn about the Swaminarayan tradition.</li><li>Experience the spiritual atmosphere and devotional activities.</li><li>Participate in religious festivals and celebrations.</li></ul><p><br></p><h2><strong>How to Reach Sarangpur</strong></h2><p><strong>By Road:</strong> Sarangpur is well-connected by road to major cities across Gujarat.</p><ul><li>Botad – Approximately 12 km</li><li>Ahmedabad – Approximately 150 km</li><li>Bhavnagar – Approximately 90 km</li><li>Rajkot – Approximately 130 km</li></ul><p><strong>By Train:</strong> The nearest railway station is Botad Junction.</p><p><strong>By Air:</strong> The nearest airports are Ahmedabad and Bhavnagar Airports.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Nearby Attractions</strong></h2><ul><li><strong>Salangpur Swaminarayan Mandir Campus</strong> – A major spiritual center.</li><li><strong>Botad City</strong> – Known for its cultural and historical significance.</li><li><strong>Nishkalank Mahadev</strong> – A famous coastal pilgrimage site near Bhavnagar.</li><li><strong>Blackbuck National Park, Velavadar</strong> – A renowned wildlife destination.</li></ul><p><br></p><h2><strong>Travel Tips</strong></h2><ul><li>Dress modestly while visiting the temple.</li><li>Arrive early to avoid large crowds, especially on Tuesdays and Saturdays.</li><li>Follow temple guidelines and maintain decorum.</li><li>Carry water during summer months.</li><li>Check festival dates if planning a pilgrimage visit.</li></ul><p><br></p><h2><strong>Quick Facts</strong></h2><p><strong>Location:</strong> Sarangpur (Salangpur), Botad District, Gujarat</p><p><strong>Famous For:</strong> Kashtabhanjan Hanuman Temple</p><p><strong>Type:</strong> Religious and Pilgrimage Destination</p><p><strong>Best Season:</strong> October to March</p><p><strong>Ideal For:</strong> Pilgrims, families, spiritual seekers, cultural travelers</p><p><strong>Google Maps:</strong> https://maps.google.com/?q=Kashtabhanjan+Hanuman+Temple+Sarangpur+Gujarat</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Final Thoughts</strong></h2><p>Sarangpur is one of Gujarat's most important spiritual destinations, offering devotees a place of faith, peace, and devotion. The sacred Kashtabhanjan Hanuman Temple, combined with its serene atmosphere and rich religious heritage, makes Sarangpur a must-visit pilgrimage site for anyone seeking spiritual fulfillment and divine blessings.</p>
