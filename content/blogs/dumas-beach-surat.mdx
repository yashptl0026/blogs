## Content (English)

<h2><strong>Dumas Beach: Surat's Mysterious Black Sand Coastal Escape</strong></h2><p>Located along the Arabian Sea coastline, <strong>Dumas Beach</strong> is one of the most popular tourist attractions near Surat, Gujarat. Famous for its unique black sand, scenic sunsets, sea breeze, and local street food, the beach attracts thousands of visitors throughout the year.</p><p>Just a short drive from Surat city, Dumas Beach offers the perfect getaway for families, couples, photographers, and nature lovers looking to relax by the sea and enjoy Gujarat's coastal charm.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Why Visit Dumas Beach?</strong></h2><ul><li>One of Gujarat's most famous beaches.</li><li>Unique black-colored sand shoreline.</li><li>Spectacular sunset views over the Arabian Sea.</li><li>Popular destination for evening walks and relaxation.</li><li>Famous for local snacks and Surat street food.</li></ul><p><br></p><h2><strong>About Dumas Beach</strong></h2><p>Dumas Beach is situated approximately 20 kilometers southwest of Surat city and serves as one of the region's most visited recreational destinations. The beach is known for its wide shoreline, cool sea breeze, and peaceful atmosphere during mornings and evenings.</p><p>Over the years, Dumas has become a favorite weekend destination for locals and tourists alike, offering a refreshing break from the city's busy lifestyle.</p><p>The beach is especially popular during sunset when the sky transforms into vibrant shades of orange, pink, and gold.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Main Attractions</strong></h2><h3><strong>Black Sand Beach</strong></h3><p>Dumas Beach is famous for its distinctive dark-colored sand, which sets it apart from many other beaches along India's coastline.</p><h3><strong>Sunset Views</strong></h3><p>The beach offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea, making it one of the best places near Surat to watch the sunset.</p><h3><strong>Sea Breeze and Walking Promenade</strong></h3><p>Visitors can enjoy long walks along the shoreline while experiencing the refreshing coastal breeze.</p><h3><strong>Local Food Stalls</strong></h3><p>The area is known for delicious local snacks, including bhajiya, pav bhaji, roasted corn, sandwiches, and traditional Surat street food.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Best Time to Visit</strong></h2><h3><strong>October - March</strong></h3><p>Pleasant weather makes this the ideal season for sightseeing, beach walks, and outdoor activities.</p><h3><strong>Monsoon (June - September)</strong></h3><p>The Arabian Sea appears dramatic during the monsoon, offering beautiful views and refreshing weather.</p><p><strong>Recommended Period:</strong> October to March.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Things to Do at Dumas Beach</strong></h2><ul><li>Watch a beautiful sunset over the Arabian Sea.</li><li>Take a relaxing walk along the beach.</li><li>Enjoy local street food and snacks.</li><li>Capture stunning coastal photographs.</li><li>Spend quality time with family and friends.</li><li>Experience Surat's famous seaside atmosphere.</li></ul><p><br></p><h2><strong>How to Reach Dumas Beach</strong></h2><p><strong>By Road:</strong> Dumas Beach is easily accessible from Surat via well-maintained roads and public transport.</p><ul><li>Surat Railway Station – Approximately 21 km</li><li>Surat Airport – Approximately 8 km</li><li>Hazira – Approximately 30 km</li><li>Athwa Gate – Approximately 18 km</li></ul><p><strong>By Train:</strong> Surat Railway Station is the nearest major railway station.</p><p><strong>By Air:</strong> Surat Airport is located just a short drive from the beach.</p><p><br></p><h2><strong>Nearby Attractions</strong></h2><ul><li><strong>Suvali Beach</strong> – A peaceful coastal destination near Surat.</li><li><strong>VR Surat</strong> – One of the city's premier shopping and entertainment hubs.</li><li><strong>Sarthana Nature Park</strong> – A popular family attraction.</li><li><strong>Dutch Garden</strong> – A historic landmark showcasing Surat's colonial heritage.</li><li><strong>Science Centre Surat</strong> – An educational and recreational attraction.</li></ul><p><br></p><h2><strong>Travel Tips</strong></h2><ul><li>Visit during
